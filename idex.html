About Us

Duolingo History

Duolingo, founded by Luis von Ahn and Severin Hacker in 2011, started as a platform to provide free language education to users worldwide.Duolingo’s unique approach uses gamification to make language learning engaging, incorporating elements like points, levels, and streaks. Its model relies on user contributions to improve the platform, such as translating real-world texts through their translation exercises.Duolingo expanded its offerings, introducing additional languages,and a mobile app. Today, it serves millions of users globally, making it one of the leading language learning tools. In recent years, Duolingo has continued to innovate, adding new features such as Duolingo Plus, a premium subscription offering an ad-free experience, offline access, and additional learning tools. The platform also launched Duolingo for Schools, aimed at educators to integrate language learning into their curricula. Additionally, Duolingo has ventured into AI and machine learning to personalize learning experiences, enhance language instruction. Its focus on expanding language offerings incorporating advanced technology has solidified its position as a leading language learning platform.
